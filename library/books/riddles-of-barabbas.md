# Riddles of Barabbas

**A Line-by-Line Rhyming Verse Translation of John's Gospel**

## Binary File Information

- **Filename**: `riddles-of-barabbas.pdf`

## Work Overview

"Riddles of Barabbas" represents a complete translation of John's Gospel where every line has been rendered into rhyming verse. This is not a poetic paraphrase, but a disciplined line-by-line translation where the rhyme constraint functions as both a therapeutic anchor and an interpretive heuristic.

## Hermeneutical Method

### Rhyme as Interpretive Constraint

The rhyme structure serves multiple functions:
1. **Therapeutic anchor**: Provides structured focus during creative work
2. **Interpretive heuristic**: Forces close attention to word choice and meaning
3. **Pattern revelation**: The constraint reveals semantic and thematic patterns not visible in prose translation
4. **Memorable form**: Makes the Gospel text more retainable through rhythmic structure

### Translation Approach

- **Line-by-line fidelity**: Each verse of John's Gospel receives careful attention
- **Rhyme scheme**: Consistent rhyming patterns throughout the 90 pages
- **Theological preservation**: Maintains doctrinal content while meeting poetic constraints
- **Readability**: Balances accuracy with natural English expression

## Mental Health Context

This work was completed without major bipolar episodes, which represents a significant accomplishment given the author's mental health journey. The rhyme constraint served therapeutic purposes:

- **Structure during instability**: The rigid form provided stability during periods that could have destabilized into mania or depression
- **Productive channeling**: Creative energy found productive outlet within defined boundaries
- **Completion achievement**: Finishing a 90-page work demonstrates sustained focus
