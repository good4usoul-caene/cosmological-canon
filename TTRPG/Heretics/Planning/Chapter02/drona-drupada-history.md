# Kingdom Origins & The Fire Adoption Ceremony

## 1. The Two Kings: Drupada & Drona

**King Drupada:**  
Once absolute ruler, Drupada now wears the wounds of betrayal and restoration. His rule is ceremonial, his pride undiminished but haunted by loss.  
*Appearance*: Silvered hair braided with snake emblems; robes of sunset gold. His eyes burn with a father's ambition and a courtier's dignity.

**Brahmin Drona:**  
Once temple priest, risen by martial cunning and politics. Drona orchestrated Drupada‚Äôs kidnapping, splitting the kingdom‚Äînow he rules half, as cleric and noble.  
*Appearance*: Saffron tunic over steel, fingers ink-stained from prophecy scrolls. He is severe, but masks emotion behind ritual.  
*Court Rivalry*: Each king now claims legitimacy; their agents and legacies overlap in every courtly intrigue.

## 2. Callisto: Court Astrologer & Schemer

**Background**:  
Callisto, royal court astrologer, is summoned by both Drupada and Drona to read signs, answer auguries, and plan omens.  
**Allegiance**:  
While formally neutral, Callisto subtly favors Drupada‚Äîoffering him more actionable prophecies. Frequently plays the kings against each other with ambiguous counsel, making himself indispensable.

## 3. The Kingdom's Fractured History

- The whole kingdom once belonged to Drupada.
- During a period of weakness (illness, betrayal?), Drona kidnapped Drupada and won dominion as priest-king.
- After intrigue and reconciliation (or ransom), Drona restored half the kingdom.
- The lands now remain officially divided, but the restoration is ceremonial‚Äîold wounds fester beneath civil propriety.

## 4. How the Adoption Ceremony Was Conceived

- Drupada, desperate to reestablish his lineage, turns to Callisto for advice.
- Callisto, using astrology and forbidden tarot readings, proposes a symbolic adoption‚Äîechoing ancient myth, fulfilling prophecy.
- The signs (constellation scrolls, particular Tarot cards) align, marking Elara Keene and a yet-unseen candidate (Dhrishtadyumna analog) as destined adoptees.
- Rumor spreads: the court prepares for a fire-lit adoption, seeking to evoke mythic favor from gods, spirits, and the crowd.

## 5. Role of PCs & Candidate Selection

- Callisto employs PC Lys Whisper (messenger, clairvoyant) to locate ideal candidates for the adoption:  
  - Elara Keene is the clear Draupadi analog‚Äîsensual bard, marked by prophecy.  
  - Dhrishtadyumna‚Äôs role may be filled by a new NPC (perhaps secretive, tiefling-marked, or an outsider hero).
  - Aldric and Corra may be interviewed or tested, each reflecting aspects of the prophecy.  
- Candidates are evaluated by omens, performance, battle, promise, or secret test.

## 6. Staging the Illusion: Born of Fire

**Performance Plan**  
- A massive bonfire is built in the palace courtyard or temple square.
- Candidates are kept hidden, masked in ash or cloaked in shadow.
- **Illusionists, bards, and Callisto himself stage a ritual:** at the ceremony‚Äôs height, candidates emerge as if from the flame‚Äîhorns aglow, skin shimmering, garbed in elemental grandeur.
- *Prophecy is intoned*: the crowd, spellbound, believes in miraculous birth/adoption.
- Special effects: magical pyrotechnics, mirrors, bardic illusions, tactical use of shadow and voice.

**Target Audience**  
- Nobles, priests, merchant guilds, and common folk all attend.
- Drona may send spies or saboteurs, hoping to disrupt or expose the ritual.
- The scene may escalate‚Äîjealous rivals, political adversaries, or mythic interference.
