    - 2: High Priestess
    - 3: The Emperor
    - 5: The Heirophant
    - 7: The Chariot 
    - 11: Justice (Associated with the Clerisy)
    - 13: Death (Associated with Pascal's Heaven)
    - 17: The Star: Forbidden:  (Sometimes associated with Lucifer; The morning star)
