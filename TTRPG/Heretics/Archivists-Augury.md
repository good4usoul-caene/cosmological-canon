## ‚öôÔ∏è Monodrone Stat Block (CR 1/8)

*Small construct, lawful neutral*

**Armor Class** 15 (natural armor)  
**Hit Points** 5 (1d6 + 2)  
**Speed** 30 ft., fly 30 ft.

**STR** 10 (+0) | **DEX** 13 (+1) | **CON** 12 (+1)  
**INT** 4 (-3) | **WIS** 10 (+0) | **CHA** 5 (-3)

**Senses** truesight 120 ft., passive Perception 10  
**Languages** Modron  
**Challenge** 1/8 (25 XP)

**Axiomatic Mind.** The monodrone can't be compelled to act in a manner contrary to its nature or its instructions.

**Disintegration.** If the monodrone dies, its body disintegrates into dust, leaving behind its weapons and anything else it was carrying.

**Actions**

***Dagger.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 3 (1d4 + 1) piercing damage.

***Javelin.*** *Ranged Weapon Attack:* +3 to hit, range 30/120 ft., one target. *Hit:* 4 (1d6 + 1) piercing damage.

**Augural Manifestation (Special)**

These monodrones are manifested by Aldra's augury ritual in response to Aldric's doubt. Each one represents one of the three Augural Principles:

- **Monodrone 1** embodies "Proof of a Crime may mean Proof of a Concept"
- **Monodrone 2** embodies "Faith must have Hope"  
- **Monodrone 3** embodies "Without Branches, a tree cannot grow or take root"

Each monodrone blocks Aldric's path. To dismiss a monodrone, Aldric must either:
- **Accept the principle** it represents (speak acknowledgment, takes 1 action) ‚Äî monodrone fades peacefully
- **Reject the principle** and destroy the monodrone through combat (standard combat)

Each interaction (acceptance or combat) takes approximately one round, delaying Aldric for three rounds total while the other PCs can act.

## üé≠ Encounter Flow

### Setup Phase
- **Location**: Near the Riven Stage side entrance, Thornehold
- **Cover Story**: Aldra is busking as a fortune-teller; Elara provides musical accompaniment
- **Crowd**: Moderate evening crowd provides cover but also witnesses
- **Lookout**: Corra positioned to watch for clerisy patrols

### Phase 1: The Negotiation (5-10 minutes)
- Lys arrives with the Eight Prime Arcana (concealed in their satchel with hidden compartments)
- Aldra and Lys begin examining the artifacts while Elara maintains augury with Legion
- All artifacts are laid out on the reading table (tarot cards and zodiac scrolls)
- This is the critical moment of vulnerability ‚Äî evidence is visible

### Phase 2: The Arrival (when tension is highest)
- Aldric Vale arrives with two clerisy inquisitors
- They've been following reports of forbidden divination in Thornehold
- Aldric recognizes the forbidden artifacts immediately (Justice card, The Star card, zodiac imagery)
- The inquisitors move to arrest Lys and Elara

### Phase 3: The Augural Response (3 rounds)
- As Aldric moves to intervene, Aldra's continuing augury manifests
- Three Monodrones materialize, each blocking Aldric's path in sequence
- Aldric must choose for each one: accept the principle or fight the modron
- Each choice takes one round, giving other PCs time to act

**PC Options During This Phase:**
- **Corra**: Engage the clerisy inquisitors, protect Lys/Elara, or help Aldric
- **Lys**: Grab the artifacts and flee through Veilstep Alley (they know the escape routes)
- **Elara**: Continue the augury, use social magic, or retreat
- **Aldric**: Must deal with each modron (accept or fight), then choose whether to arrest or let the heretics go

### Phase 4: Resolution
Multiple possible outcomes based on PC choices and Aldric's decisions.
