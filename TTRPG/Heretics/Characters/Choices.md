## ‚ö†Ô∏è Act I Spell Notes (copy into session doc)
- For Act I, avoid casting spells that produce obvious sensory spectacle in public (bright flashes, large elemental effects, dramatic summoning).  
- Allowed: subtle enchantments, social/mental influence spells, quiet divination, and ritual-cast spells with no outward spectacle. The GM rules edge cases.  
- God-Invocation flavor:
  - Pascal‚Äôs God: divination returns comforting platitudes (mechanically inert unless GM grants roleplay benefit).
